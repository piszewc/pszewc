So many books, so little time

Below you can find list of resources you can use to learn programming, data science, business and psychology.

Technical Learning:

EDX Programming Using Python: This is the best course on introductory python I have seen and got me going. It is a time-consuming course, but the problems they give are in depth, well-explained, and organized thoroughly. Some of the questions are HARD, but I think that is better than the opposite of everything being too simplified.

Career:

The Startup Owner's Manual: One of the best books for startups in addition to "Lean Startup". Steve Blank makes a lot of very good points about startup growth and culture, i.e. what makes startups succeed and what makes others fail. He also goes into significant depth on what you and your business can do to minimise the risk of failure.

Mindset:

The ONE Thing A great book on time management and productivity. This books is a must read for anyone who feels overwhelmed by too many things to do on their daily schedule.
